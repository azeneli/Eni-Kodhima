I am Eni Kodhima

Architect + Visual Designer in NYC

Experienced in project managing residential and mix use architecture and development of high quality 3D Visualizations.  I was introduced to Architecture very early on when I attended High School of Art & Design. It became apparent to me that it was my calling and I continued to pursue my studies at New York City College of Technology where I sharpened my skills and explored my creative capabilities.  My attention to detail and passion for beauty are reflected throughout my designs. I love mixing styles by creating comfortable spaces that embody elegance, balance and the human spirit. Above all, I aim to convey a clear image of life by the simple form.
